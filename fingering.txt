HF: relatively higher finger number
LF: relatively lower finger number

HF may not be on a lower fret than LF

HF and LF may be on the same fret if HF is on lower string number (higher pitched string)

finger 1 may play multiple notes on the same fret

adjacent fingers may not be spaced more than 2 frets apart

total fret span may not be greater than 5 frets
